Office Hours Announcer

Thinkful.com mentors wanted a way to easily remind students when online office hours were beginning, and how to join them. Using three separate APIs to access the required services, I created an integration that allows a mentor in a Google Hangouts window to load data from Google Spreadsheets, then to post relevant messages based on that data to the student Slack channels. As a useful feature, the app can automatically repeat its announcement every fifteen minutes while office hours is going on.
