About me

I'm a designer and developer living in the Mid Cities between Dallas and Fort Worth, Texas. I get inspired by photography, art, urban life, novels, beautiful code, and old issues of National Geographic Magazine. I've come to realize that my best days are often marked by a combination of learning, solving tough problems, and teaching others, and it's the pursuit of that that led me first to printing and creative design, and second to code and the creation of new experiences on the Web.

Below, you may browse my recent projects and learn more about my capabilities. After that, I'd love to hear from you. Tell me what excites you and what you're working on. I'm always eager to meet new people, to hear provocative opinions and to share a desire to understand the world around. Connect with me here.
